Three-or-more framings are enforced. Without alternatives, the value collapses to a binary.

Questions are first-class state. The Mind holds them until evidence, inquiry, or human input resolves them.

An interest is something the Mind has noticed but not acted on. Interests decay unless reinforced.

A curiosity is intent-driven, distinct from an interest (anomaly-driven).

Opportunities are framed by what they enable for the counterpart, not what they extract. Kindfulness vector at work.
